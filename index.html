About the Game

Finally, a merge game that's actually about something

Ruby’s Mystery: A Merge Story connects item merging with storytelling.
Players combine objects to reveal new clues, restore environments, and move forward through a layered mystery.
You're not just mindlessly merging stuff - every move actually pushes the story forward.

How To Play

Combine, discover, progress

  • Merge Items

    Drag and combine identical objects to upgrade them.

  • Unlock Clues

    Higher-level items reveal parts of the story.

  • Restore Locations

    Use items to repair and explore new areas.

  • Complete Tasks

    Follow objectives to guide your progress.

  • Advance the Story

    Piece together the mystery step by step.
